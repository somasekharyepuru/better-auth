## Feature Distribution Rationale

### Free Tier Strategy
**Philosophy:** Give enough to build habit, limit enough to convert

**Why 3 priorities?**
- Research shows 3-5 is optimal for daily focus
- 3 lets users experience core value
- Wanting more = sign of engagement = ready to pay

**Why limit Pomodoro to 3 sessions?**
- ~90 minutes of focus time (enough to prove value)
- Users doing deep work daily will hit limit quickly
- Timer is key differentiator, so give taste but not full meal

**Why 2 Life Areas?**
- Lets users separate "Work" and "Personal"
- Experiencing organization value without full flexibility
- Natural upgrade path when they want "Health," "Learning," etc.

### Premium Tier Strategy
**Philosophy:** Remove all friction, unlock power tools

**Advanced Tools as premium:**
- Eisenhower Matrix: Strategic planning feature (power user)
- Decision Log: Specialized tool (not everyone needs)
- These justify "premium" label beyond just "more of the same"

**Multi-calendar sync:**
- Many professionals have work + personal calendars
- This is a B2B/professional signal
- Worth paying for if you need it

## FAQs for Pricing Page

**Q: Can I switch from monthly to annual?**  
A: Yes! We'll prorate your current month and apply it to your annual plan.

**Q: What happens if I downgrade from Premium to Free?**  
A: Your data is never deleted. You'll keep access to all history but can only add 3 priorities per day going forward.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**  
A: Yes, 30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked.

**Q: Can I try Premium before paying?**  
A: After using Daymark for 7 days, we'll offer you a free 14-day Premium trial with no credit card required.

**Q: Do prices ever increase?**
